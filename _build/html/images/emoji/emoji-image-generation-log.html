Invisibles general¶

Two options for VS15 and VS16 are included.

Adobe Source Emoji includes non-color glyphs for both codepoints that offer a degree of visual communication to prevent confusion in the sequence illustrations, but they are not immediately associated with the codepoint itself, like Gentium Plus’s are.
